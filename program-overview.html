Program overview

Encore’ – official Comeback program for women on career breaks by ADITYA BIRLA GROUP. The intent of the program is to provide mutually rewarding work opportunities to qualified Indian women who are out of the workforce due to various reasons.
Encore’ offers live projects with flexible workplace arrangements wherever possible where the participants use their expertise to solve real business problems. You will work closely with the project sponsors of the identified projects who will also be their mentors during the course of the project.
The program is an effective interim platform that enables you to slowly transition back into a workplace environment. The participants can also subsequently look at exploring possible full-time work opportunities across ABG's Apparel & Retail businesses in consultation with their project mentors.
