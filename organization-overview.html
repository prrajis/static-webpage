Organization overview

A US $40 billion corporation, the Aditya Birla Group is in the league of Fortune 500 companies. It is anchored by an extraordinary force of over 120,000 employees belonging to 42 nationalities. The Group has been ranked No. 4 in the global 'Top Companies for Leaders' survey and ranked No. 1 in the Asia Pacific for 2011. 'Top Companies for Leaders' is the most comprehensive study of organisational leadership in the world conducted by Aon Hewitt, Fortune Magazine, and RBL (a strategic HR and Leadership Advisory firm).
Over 50 percent of the Aditya Birla Group's revenues flow from its overseas operations. The Group operates in 35 countries - Australia, Austria, Bangladesh, Brazil, Canada, China, Egypt, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Ivory Coast, Japan, Korea, Laos, Luxembourg, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Poland, Russia, Singapore, South Africa, Spain, Sri Lanka, Sweden, Switzerland, Tanzania, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, UK, USA, and Vietnam.
